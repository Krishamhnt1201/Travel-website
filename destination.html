Discover Your Dream Destinations

Travel guides for France, USA, Japan, and Switzerland

France: Paris – The City of Light and Romance

Paris beckons with its Eiffel Tower glow, Seine River charm, and endless croissants. Focus on the heart of it all: the City of Love.

Top Hotels

  • Cheval Blanc Paris: A riverside luxury haven with Michelin-starred dining and spa bliss (from €1,200/night).
  • Ritz Paris: Timeless opulence on Place Vendôme, perfect for high tea and Hemingway vibes (from €1,500/night).
  • Park Hyatt Paris-Vendôme: Modern luxury with a serene courtyard spa (from €900/night).
  • Hotel Dame des Arts: Chic Left Bank boutique with rooftop views (from €500/night).

Shopping Places

  • Galeries Lafayette Paris Haussmann
  • Le Bon Marché Rive Gauche
  • Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré
  • Marché aux Puces de Saint-Ouen

USA: New York City – The Big Apple Buzz

NYC pulses with skyscrapers, street food, and Broadway magic. Dive into Manhattan's energy.

Top Hotels

  • The Carlyle: Upper East Side legend blending Art Deco glamour with Bemelmans Bar jazz nights (from $1,200/night).
  • The Fifth Avenue Hotel: Eclectic NoMad jewel set in a historic mansion with bold interiors and Michelin-worthy dining (from $950/night).
  • The Whitby Hotel: Vibrant Midtown boutique by Kit Kemp, offering whimsical design and afternoon tea near MoMA (from $700/night).
  • The Langham, New York: Sleek Fifth Avenue sanctuary with spacious suites, Chuan Spa, and fine Italian dining (from $1,000/night).

Shopping Places

  • Fifth Avenue
  • SoHo
  • Chelsea Market
  • Canal Street

Japan: Tokyo – Neon Dreams and Tranquil Temples

Tokyo fuses futuristic vibes with ancient traditions. Explore Shibuya's scramble and serene shrines.

Top Hotels

  • Aman Tokyo: Urban serenity above the skyline, with minimalist Zen design and a world-class spa (from ¥150,000/night).
  • Bvlgari Hotel: Italian glamour meets Japanese sophistication, soaring high above Tokyo Midtown Yaesu (from ¥200,000/night).
  • The Capitol Hotel: Refined retreat near the Imperial Palace, with Japanese elegance and sweeping city views (from ¥80,000/night).
  • Park Hotel: Art-inspired haven in Shiodome, where each room doubles as a canvas with city and Tokyo Tower views (from ¥60,000/night).

Shopping Places

  • Ginza
  • Shibuya
  • Akihabara
  • Harajuku

Switzerland: Zurich – Alpine Charm Meets Urban Cool

Zurich offers lakeside leisure and chocolate-fueled hikes. Stroll the Limmat River.

Top Hotels

  • The Dolder Grand: Iconic hilltop palace blending historic grandeur with panoramic lake and Alps views (from CHF 1,200/night).
  • Baur au Lac: Timeless lakeside elegance with Michelin-starred dining and private gardens in the heart of Zurich (from CHF 1,100/night).
  • Park Hyatt Zurich: Modern Swiss luxury with serene spa, contemporary design, and prime shopping district access (from CHF 900/night).
  • 25hours Hotel Zurich West: Quirky industrial-chic vibe with bold interiors and rooftop bar overlooking the city (from CHF 250/night).

Shopping Places

  • Bahnhofstrasse
  • Sihlcity
  • Old Town Boutiques
  • Flohmarkt Bürkliplatz
